# Privacy Policy - Domain Switcher

**Last Updated:** November 6, 2024

## Overview

Domain Switcher is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how the extension handles your information.

## Data Collection

**Domain Switcher does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

## Local Storage Only

The extension stores the following information **locally on your device only** using Chrome's storage API:
- Target domain configuration (e.g., "localhost")
- Target port configuration (e.g., "8000")
- HTTPS preference (enabled/disabled)

This information:
- Never leaves your device
- Is not sent to any external servers
- Is not shared with third parties
- Is not used for analytics or tracking

## Permissions Used

The extension requests the following Chrome permissions:

### `tabs` Permission
**Purpose:** To read the URL of the current tab so the extension can generate the switched domain URL.
**Data Access:** Current tab URL only
**Data Usage:** Used only to construct the target URL, never stored or transmitted

### `storage` Permission
**Purpose:** To save your domain configuration preferences locally on your device
**Data Stored:** Domain, port, and HTTPS preference only
**Data Location:** Local device only (Chrome's sync storage)

## Third-Party Services

Domain Switcher does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools.

## Data Security

Since no data is collected or transmitted, there is no risk of data breaches related to external servers. Your configuration is stored securely using Chrome's built-in storage API.

## Children's Privacy

Domain Switcher does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The extension is designed for developers and does not collect personal information from any users.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.

## Your Rights

Since Domain Switcher does not collect any personal data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete. You can remove all locally stored preferences by uninstalling the extension.

**In Summary:** Domain Switcher is a simple tool that helps you switch URLs. It stores your preferences locally on your device and does nothing else. Your privacy is fully protected.
